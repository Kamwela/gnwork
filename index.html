About Us

Welcome to GNWORK Company Limited , your one-stop solution for professional automotive services and training. With Three years of experience and a passion for vehicles, we are committed to delivering high-quality repairs, maintenance, and educational programs to meet the needs of car owners, enthusiasts, and future drivers.
At GNWORK Garage, we specialize in:
- **Engine Repairs** and maintenance to keep your vehicle running smoothly.
- **Gearbox Solutions** for all types of vehicles.
- **Electrical Systems Diagnostics** to resolve complex issues.
- **Injector Pump (Nozzle) Services** for efficiency and performance.
- **Air Conditioning (AC) Repairs** to ensure comfort on the road.
- **Full Diagnosis Services** using advanced tools for accuracy.
- **Tinted Windows** for style, privacy, and protection.
- **Body Work** including **coloring** and **polishing** for a fresh look.
- **Comprehensive Car Checkups** for safety and reliability.
- **Car Assembly Expertise** to bring vehicles to life.
Beyond repairs, we are dedicated to **empowering individuals** through our hands-on training programs:
- Learn **basic vehicle repairs** for minor fixes.
- Understand **routine vehicle checks** to maintain your car’s health.
- Master **driving techniques** for safety and efficiency.
- Gain insights into **driving management** for better vehicle handling.
Our mission is to provide top-tier automotive care while equipping our clients with the **knowledge and skills** to manage their vehicles effectively. Whether you’re looking to repair, maintain, or learn, GNWORK Garage is here to drive you forward.
